Collect Flags - Avoid the sea hole

Movement Program:

How to play

  • Collect flags to earn 100 points, and avoid to be caught by the sea hole
  • Choose five movements to move the boat, and press the button Run Movements
  • You can use one Hole Cover per turn, press the letter “C”
  • Use the arrow keyboard to indicate the direction
  • If a sea hole is covered you will earn 150 points, and if a sea hole catches a flag you lose 50 points
